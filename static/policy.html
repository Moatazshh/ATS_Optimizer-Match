Privacy Policy

At ATS Optimizer, we value your privacy and the confidentiality of your professional data. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Information Collection

We process the following data when you use our service:

  • Resume Files: To perform analysis and optimization.
  • Job Descriptions: To match your profile against specific requirements.
  • Contact Info: Names and email addresses found in resumes are used solely for the generation of the optimized document.

2. Data Processing & AI

Your data is processed using Large Language Models (LLMs) to provide semantic reconstruction and keyword optimization. We do not use your personal data to train these models.

3. Data Retention

We believe in "Privacy by Design". Files uploaded to our server are processed in-memory and are not permanently stored on our disks unless explicitly required for the session. Temporary files are purged regularly.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use secure API providers (such as OpenAI or Anthropic) to power our AI features. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and do not use your data for their own purposes.
